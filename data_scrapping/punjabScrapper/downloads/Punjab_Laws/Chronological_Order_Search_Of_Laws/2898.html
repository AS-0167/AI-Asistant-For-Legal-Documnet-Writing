[1]THE PUNJAB REVISION OF SALARIES OF PUBLIC REPRESENTATIVES ACT 2024

 

(ACT XVI OF 2024)

 

[20th December 2024]

An Act further to revise the salaries of the holders of public representative offices of Province of the Punjab.

It is necessary further to revise the salaries of holders of public representative offices of Province of the Punjab for the purposes hereinafter appearing.

Be it enacted by Provincial Assembly of the Punjab as follows:

1. Short title and commencement. (1) This Act may be cited as the Punjab Revision of Salaries of Public Representatives Act 2024.

(2) It shall come into force at once.

 

2. Amendment of Act XVI of 1974. In the Punjab Provincial Assembly (Salaries, Allowances and Privileges of Members) Act 1974 (XVI of 1974), in section 3:

(a)                in sub-section (1), for the words seventy six, the words four hundred shall be substituted;

(b)               for sub-section (4), the following shall be substituted:

(4) The Government may, by notification in the official Gazette, increase the salary mentioned in subsection (1).; and

(c)                Explanation existing at the end shall be omitted.
