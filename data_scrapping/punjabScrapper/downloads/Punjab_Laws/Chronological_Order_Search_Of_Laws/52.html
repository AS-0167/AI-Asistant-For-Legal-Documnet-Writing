[1]THE REGISTRATION (PUNJAB AMENDMENT) ACT 1941

(Punjab Act VIII of 1941)

[23 May 1941]

An Act to amend the [2]Indian Registration Act, 1908, in its application to the Punjab

It is hereby enacted as follows:-

1.   Short title.— This Act may be called the [3]Indian Registration (Punjab Amendment) Act, 1911.

2.   Amendment of section 22 of Act XVI of 1908.— The Indian Registration Act, 1908 shall, in its application to the Punjab, be amended as follows:-

      In sub-section (1) of section 22 of the said Act, the words “not being houses in towns” shall be omitted.

 


[1]Received the assent of the Governor-General on the 9th May 1911, and was first published in the Government Gazette, Punjab, dated 23rd May, 1941.

[2]Now the Registration Act, 1908.

[3]Ibid.
